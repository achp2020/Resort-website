Travel Arrangements

Makuti resort is situated 400 kilometers from Nairobi and can be accessed by either air or road transport. There are charter flights from nairobi's Wilson airport to Masai mara and the flight time is only 25 miniutes. Should you choose to fly kindly contact us to arrange pick service from the airstrip to our resort. Alternatively should you opt to use the road, we have very comfortable land cruiser and travel guides that can pick you from your hotel in Nairobi and drive you to our resort. Ideally this takes 4 hours only.
