Accomodation page

Spending the night away in Masai Mara, the best wildlife conservancy in the world is truly an thrilling experience. Makuti Resort offers uniquely designed luxurious “tents” with very large verandas offering infinite vantage views of the Mara plains. The cottages are all en-suite bathrooms with large mirrors, WC, hot and cold running water and showers, and contain fully stocked mini bars.

Makuti resort provides various accomodation options to accomodate our guests'pocket and numbers as follows;

  • Standard tents
  • Deluxe tents
  • Club tents
  • Family suite
