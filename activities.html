Activities page

Makuti Resort is in the heart of Mara conservancy surrounded by shrubs and exotic trees and near the mighty Mara river. The rivers draining into the Mara river are breeding grounds for local fish hence the guests can spend time fishing. The exotic forests are a bird sanctuary supporting indigenous birds of various species offering the guests a vantage spot for bird watching. A few kilometers from our resort are the Masai villages.We organise day trip to these villages where the guests get a life time opportunity to indulge in the Masai culture.

Our resort is further well-equiped with safari vehicles and excellent guides. Hence in a position to provide our guests with countless safari experience. Other activities that we are able to offer include

  • Conferencing
  • Dining
  • Swimming
  • Spa
